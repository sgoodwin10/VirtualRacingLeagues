Design 3: Refined Slate

Aesthetic: A more sophisticated, bluish-slate dark theme with significantly lighter elements. Less raw industrial, more polished tech.

Changes from Original:

  • Slate-blue undertones instead of pure gray (#121418, #1e2128 vs #0a0a0a, #141414)
  • Much lighter surface colors - creating more visible contrast
  • Generous border radius throughout (xl and 2xl)
  • Ambient gradient background with subtle orange/blue tones
  • Platform tags displayed as individual chips
  • Pill-style badges instead of squared
  • Glass-effect navigation with blur
  • Shine effect on primary buttons
  • Gradient stat cards with subtle border highlights
  • Row hover shows accent bar on left edge
  • Glow animation on online status indicator

Best For: Users who want a refined, premium dark interface that feels modern and polished rather than raw industrial
